About Stu-SoulCare

Stu-SoulCare is a student-focused emotional support companion for exam stress, family pressure, social challenges, and daily overwhelm. It offers supportive conversation, grounding tools, and practical next-step guidance in a safe, low-pressure space.

This is not a licensed therapy service. If you may be in danger, use Emergency Help immediately.
